## 3. Interview Questions

### Opening (5 minutes)
- Thank them for their time
- Introduce yourself and the purpose
- Request permission to record (if needed)
- Explain the process

### Role & Responsibilities (10 minutes)
1. Can you describe your current role?
2. Who do you work with on a daily basis?
3. What systems do you use?

### Current Process (15 minutes)
4. Can you describe a typical work process?
5. What does your typical workday look like?
6. How do you handle [specific task]?
7. Where is data entered and where does it come from?
8. What reports do you need regularly?

### Pain Points (15 minutes)
9. What is the biggest challenge in your current work?
10. Are there any steps that take unnecessary time?
11. Are there any errors or mistakes that occur frequently?
12. What would help make your work more efficient?

### Expectations (10 minutes)
13. What do you expect from the new system?
14. What features are most important?
15. Do you have any concerns about the project?

### Closing
16. Is there anything else you would like to share?
17. Who else should be interviewed?
18. Are there any documents you can share?
